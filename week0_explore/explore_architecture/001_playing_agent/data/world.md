# World Map Notes (Midgaard)

## Route from starting road to Bakery
1. Start: A Road Through The Plains (exits: e, w) — east toward Midgaard
2. Outside The West Gate Of Midgaard (exits: e, w)
3. Inside The West Gate Of Midgaard (exits: e, s, w) — cityguard here
4. Main Street [west end] (exits: n, e, s, w) — S: Guild of Magic Users, N: magic shop, W: city gate
5. Main Street [bakery block] (exits: n, e, s, w) — S: Armory, N: **The Bakery**, E: Market Square
6. -> go N into The Bakery (exit: s only) — cityguard + baker NPC here

Market Square (exits: n, e, s, w) — N: temple square, S: common square, E/W: main street
Further east on Main Street: general store (N), Pet Shop (S), a Peacekeeper NPC stands here.

## The Bakery
- Shop room, run by "the baker" NPC.
- Commands: `list` (see menu), `buy <item>` (purchase), `read sign` (shows instructions)
