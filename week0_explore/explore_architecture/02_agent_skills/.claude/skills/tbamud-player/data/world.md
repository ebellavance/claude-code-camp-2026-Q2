# Midgaard World Notes

Map knowledge accumulated across play sessions. Market Square is the central
hub; everything below is described relative to it unless noted otherwise.

## Market Square (hub)
- N: Temple Square
- S: Common Square
- E: Main Street (east) -> weapon shop, Guild of Swordsmen, town exit
- W: Main Street (west) -> Bakery, Armory, further west to the city gate

## Main Street (west branch, toward the Bakery)
- Market Square (E) <-> Main Street [Bakery to the N, Armory to the S] <-> Main Street (end)
- **The Bakery**: menu via `list` — danish pastry 7g, bread 14g, waybread 70g (all unlimited stock)
- **The Armory**: armor/weapon shop, dead end (only exit N back to Main Street)
- Main Street (end): magic shop to the N (unvisited), **Guild of Magic Users** to the S,
  Inside The West Gate to the W
- Inside The West Gate -> S -> Wall Road, which continues south several rooms
  toward a river bridge. Not fully explored past the bridge approach.

## Main Street (east branch, toward the Guild of Swordsmen)
- Market Square (E) <-> Main Street [general store N, Pet Shop S] <-> Main Street
  [weapon shop N, **Guild of Swordsmen** S, town exit E]
- **Guild of Swordsmen** (dummy's guild — Warrior/Swordpupil):
  Entrance Hall (knight + cityguard) --E--> Bar of Swordsmen --S--> Tournament
  And Practice Yard, where the guildmaster stands. `practice <skill>` only
  works while standing in the Practice Yard. The yard also has a well leading
  `down` into darkness — unexplored, possibly a dungeon/arena.

## Temple Square (N of Market Square)
- W: **Clerics' Guild** (unvisited interior)
- E: Grunting Boar Inn — Entrance Hall (post office N, bar E, reception up).
  Reception has a Cryogenic Center to the N (this is the `rent`/save-and-quit
  desk, not a guild).
- N: Temple of Midgaard -> further N: By The Temple Altar -> N leads out to
  the countryside (leaving the city). Temple also has a Reading Room (W) and
  donation room (E) off the main hall, unvisited.

## Common Square (S of Market Square)
- W: Poor Alley (beggar at the west end, dead-ends at the city wall). South
  branch off Poor Alley leads to the Grubby Inn — unvisited.
- E: Dark Alley (mercenaries loiter here) -> S: **Guild of Thieves** (entrance
  hall only visited; not dummy's guild, `practice` refused here).
  Dark Alley continues E to Dark Alley At The Levee -> E: Eastern End Of The
  Alley -> S: Deserted Warehouse (sailor NPC). Dark Alley At The Levee -> S:
  The Levee (river, boats, a retired captain selling them).
- S: The Dump -> D: sewer entrance (Quadruple Junction Under The Dump, exits
  n/e/s/w/up). Sewer maze beyond this junction is unexplored.

## Monsters seen so far (none fought yet)
- beastly fido — common around Common Square / Poor Alley, scavenging garbage
- oozing green gelatinous blob — seen on Main Street
- cityguards / Peacekeepers — town guards, not hostile unless provoked
